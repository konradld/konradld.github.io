# Welcome!

I am a PhD Candidate at the [WU – Vienna University of Economics and Business](https://www.wu.ac.at/en/) currently most engaged with theoretical work in the fields of machine learning and Bayesian statistics with applications to environmental and macro economics.

My research interests are *econometric robustness* and *anomaly detection* in economic data as well as *economic risk* and modelling *dependency structures*.
